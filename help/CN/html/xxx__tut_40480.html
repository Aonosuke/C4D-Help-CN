Mechanical Modeling Tutorial

By Arndt von Koenigsmarck

In this chapter we will demonstrate technical modeling tools and techniques in practical applications. Even experienced users will benefit from the information in this chapter.

The basic element of any animation or rendered image are 3D objects, which must be modeled as a first step. This chapter is divided into the following exercises:

Working with Boole and Instance Objects
Modeling with Splines and Generator Objects
Modeling with Polygon Tools
Subdivision Surfaces Modeling
